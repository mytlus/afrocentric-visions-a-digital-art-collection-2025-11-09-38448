Afrocentric Visions offers flexible licensing options to suit various creative needs:

  • Personal Use License: Perfect for personal projects, social media, and non-commercial applications. This is our most affordable option.
  • Commercial License: Ideal for businesses, marketing agencies, and commercial projects. Includes rights for client work and business applications.
  • Extended License: Our premium option for large-scale projects, including print runs, merchandise, and digital products with high distribution numbers.
  • Custom Licensing: For unique or specialized needs, we offer custom licensing agreements. Contact our team for details.

All licenses are perpetual, meaning you can use the artwork indefinitely once purchased. We also provide clear documentation of your rights with every download.

Supporting artists is at the core of our mission. Here's how we do it:

  • Fair Revenue Sharing: Artists receive 50% of all revenue generated from their artwork downloads and licenses, ensuring they're fairly compensated for their work.

  • Community Recognition: Featured artists receive special recognition, promotion across our platforms, and opportunities for collaboration.

We believe in creating a sustainable ecosystem where artists can thrive while building a diverse, high-quality collection for our community.

We provide artwork in multiple formats and resolutions to accommodate different project needs:

  • File Formats: JPEG, PNG (with transparency), TIFF, and PSD (Photoshop) for maximum flexibility and compatibility with all design software.
  • Resolution Options: Available in multiple resolutions including 72 DPI (web), 150 DPI (screen), and 300 DPI (print quality).
  • Maximum Resolution: Premium downloads offer up to 8K resolution for large-format printing and high-end commercial applications.
  • Scalable Versions: Select artwork is available in vector format (AI, EPS) for unlimited scalability without quality loss.
  • Custom Sizes: Contact our support team for custom resolution requests for specialized projects.

All files are optimized for their intended use and include metadata with artist information and licensing details. You can download all available formats for each artwork with a single purchase.

About Afrocentric Visions

Afrocentric Visions was founded in 2022 with a simple yet powerful mission: to celebrate and amplify the voices of Afrocentric artists while providing the creative community with access to authentic, high-quality cultural artwork. Our journey began when our founders recognized a significant gap in the market for authentic Afrocentric visual content that honored cultural heritage while embracing contemporary artistic innovation. What started as a passion project has evolved into a thriving platform connecting thousands of artists, creators, and cultural enthusiasts from around the world. We've grown from a small collection of 50 artworks to over 500 carefully curated pieces, all while maintaining our commitment to authenticity, quality, and fair artist compensation.

At our core, we believe that art is a powerful vehicle for cultural expression, social change, and human connection. Our mission is to create a sustainable ecosystem where Afrocentric artists are valued, supported, and fairly compensated for their creative work. We're committed to promoting cultural pride and representation across all industries, from design and marketing to education and entertainment. Every artwork in our collection tells a story, reflects authentic cultural perspectives, and contributes to a more inclusive and diverse creative landscape. We're not just building a platformwe're building a movement that celebrates the richness of African and diaspora cultures, supports emerging artists, and empowers creators to tell meaningful stories through visual art.

Our Vision

To become the world's leading platform for authentic Afrocentric art, where artists are empowered, creators are inspired, and cultural diversity is celebrated across all creative industries.

  • Equity: Fair compensation and opportunity
